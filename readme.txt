== Description ==

Delete unwanted posts by keyword

Get rid of unwanted blogposts on your blog! How embarrassing, eh?! 

Just enter list of words and they will never see a day on your wordpress blog.

Do not allow authors to make your blog spam hole! Filter shity blog posts by keywords. 

Auto delete (immediately after author clicks 'publish') unwanted posts by list of words.

== Installation ==

1. Download the .zip file
2. Extract the zip-file, containing the folder "post-filter"
3. Upload to your wp-content/plugins/ folder on your blog
4. Log in to your blog, go to the plugins page and look for "Post Filter" and activate the plugin
5. You can now access the plugin in your "Tool" menu under "Post Filter"

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What happens to my posts? =

All of them will be completely erased from the database. 
There is no recovery option if you check to purge them from trash. 
Remember to back up your database first in case a mistake happens!
The author of this plugin is not responsible if you accidentally deleted important information - use it on your own responsibility.
